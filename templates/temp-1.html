Moment of Truth

(Free Verse Poem)

I am wondering at the corridor

Waiting for someone else to come

I sensed the environment is new

The place is as wide as a space

I think a lot of my chosen course

For it is newly to me

I am looking for someone to lean on

I am hesitant and yet downgraded

My chosen career is not my passion

It makes me weak!

I reached many hands for any help

But after the asperities

Those make me stronger;

I strive in every assessment —

Battling sleepless nights just to survive —

The pressure is on

Yes, I thought I carry the world.

A day has come to students

Bearing various reactions

To their bright and uniform grades

They deserved it, so as I

I never boast to receive those

It was not promising

But, I feel like I'm on cloud nine

'Cause I exerted efforts to achieve it

It was unexpected, absolutely!

This triumph was made easy for me

Since most people, some friends,

They have extended their hands to me.
