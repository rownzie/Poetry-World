Payback Time

(Triolet Poem)

Feeling delight for this achievement

A sentiment of enjoyment I never expected

since I remembered the hardship moment

Feeling delight for this achievement

as this day brings fulfillment

yet I never foresaw this circumstance be fleeted

Feeling delight for this achievement

A sentiment of enjoyment I never expected
