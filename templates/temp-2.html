20/20 Vision

(Free Verse Poem)

Achievement is meant for those who deserve it;

Grief - the moment of sorrow

Enjoy - the point of achievement

Anger - the moment of procrastination

Sad - the time of your rejection

What makes you survive?

is the moment you let the severe test

to penetrate your soul and mind.

Be amazed since the duration has elapsed.

This is time to cherish

Appreciate your good reputation.

It marks your hailing moment.

Reminisce, oh reminisce!

It was such great college days.
