About Poetry World

Poetry World is a website of a collection of poems. The author believes that through writing poems, we can convey our emotions and unspoken words into expressive lines. It uses symbolism and metaphors to describe what we wanted to say. Henceforth, this webpage produces several poem pieces that would also awaken others' perceptions of what art is. It also appreciates and promotes the surprisingly creative and imaginative meaning of poems.
